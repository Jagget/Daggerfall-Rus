QBN:
Item _reward_ gold range 10000 to 10000

Person _questgiver_ group Questor male

Place _mondung_ remote dungeon6

Clock _timer_ 00:00 0 flag 1 range 0 2
Clock _delay_ 8:00 12:00

Foe _boss_ is Warrior

--	Quest start-up:
	start timer _timer_
	log 1030 step 0
	pc at _mondung_ set _inside_
	place foe _boss_ at _mondung_
	reveal _mondung_

_inside_ task:
    say 1010

_daily_ task:
    daily from 0:00 to 23:59
    cast Heal spell on _boss_
    cast Shield spell on _boss_
    clear _daily_

_inj_ task:
    clicked foe _boss_
    injured _boss_
    say 1011
    change foe _boss_ infighting true

_slain_ task:
    killed _boss_
    say 1012

_qgclicked_ task:
	clicked npc _questgiver_

_finish_ task:
	when _slain_ and _qgclicked_
    say 1013
    log 1031 step 0
    start timer _delay_

_delay_ task:
	give pc _reward_
	end quest

_timer_ task:
	end quest

_clearclick_ task:
	when _qgclicked_ and not _slain_
	clear _qgclicked_ _clearclick_
