    pick one of _good_ _good_ _good_ _bad_ _good_

_bad_ task:
    pick one of _good_ _good_ _good_ _good_ _fatal_ _good_ _good_ _good_ _good_

variable _good_

_givereward_ task:
    take _faker_ from pc
    give pc _reward_
    start quest JHCAPA01passive
    end quest

variable _fatal_
_endchain_ task:
    when _fatal_ and _givereward_
    say 1010
    end quest
