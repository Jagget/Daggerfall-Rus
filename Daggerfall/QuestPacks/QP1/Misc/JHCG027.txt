    log 1030 step 0

    pc at _dung_ set _inside_

    clicked npc _qg_

_begin_ task:
    when _qgclicked_ and not _talked_ and not _found_
    say 1012

    log 1031 step 1
    start task _talked_

    say 1099
    clear _qgclicked_ _reminder_

    start quest JHCGENT4
    give pc _reward_
    end quest
